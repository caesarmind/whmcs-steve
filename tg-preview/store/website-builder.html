Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any technical skills to use the Website Builder?
No technical skills are required. Our drag-and-drop editor is designed for beginners. Simply choose a template, customize it with your content, and publish. No coding knowledge needed.
Can I use my own domain name?
Yes, the Pro and Business plans include custom domain support. You can connect a domain you already own or register a new one through our platform. Starter plans use a free subdomain.
Can I switch templates after I start building?
Yes, you can switch templates at any time. Your content will be preserved and automatically adapted to the new template layout. We recommend finalizing your template choice early for the best results.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, all Website Builder plans include a 14-day free trial. You can build and preview your site before committing. No credit card required to start your trial.
