Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about our cloud hosting.

What is cloud hosting?
Cloud hosting uses a network of virtual servers to host your website, providing better reliability and scalability than traditional single-server hosting. Your site is distributed across multiple servers, so if one goes down, others take over seamlessly.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated, so you only pay for what you use.
Do you offer free migration?
Yes! Our team will migrate your existing website from any host for free. Just submit a migration request after signing up and we'll handle the rest — usually within 24 hours.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Every hosting plan comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact support within 30 days for a full refund.
What control panel do you use?
We provide cPanel, the industry-standard hosting control panel. It includes a file manager, email accounts, database management, one-click installers, and more — all in a clean, intuitive interface.
