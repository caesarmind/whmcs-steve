Filtering questions.

Common questions about email filtering and security.

How does spam filtering work?
Our AI-powered system analyzes email headers, content, sender reputation, and behavioral patterns in real-time. Suspicious emails are quarantined for review while clean mail is delivered instantly.
Will legitimate emails get blocked?
Our false positive rate is just 0.003%. Any flagged emails go to quarantine where you can review and release them. You can also create allowlists for trusted senders.
Does it work with my current email?
Yes. Our filtering works with any email provider — simply update your MX records to route mail through our servers first. Setup takes under 5 minutes per domain.
How long is email archived?
Complete plans archive for 30 days. Enterprise plans archive for 1 year. All archived emails are searchable by sender, recipient, subject, date, or content.
