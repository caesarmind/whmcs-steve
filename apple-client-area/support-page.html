Support FAQ.

What are your support hours?

We're available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Our global support team ensures someone is always ready to help, regardless of timezone.

How fast will I get a response?

Our average response time is 15 minutes for ticket submissions. Live chat connects you instantly. Phone support has zero hold time during normal hours.

What's included in support?

All customers receive ticket support, knowledge base access, and community forum access. Pro plans add live chat and phone support with priority queue.

Can you help with migrations?

Absolutely! Our migration team handles everything — websites, databases, emails, DNS records. Just provide your old host credentials and we'll do the rest for free.

Do you offer managed services?

Yes. Our managed hosting plans include server administration, security patching, performance tuning, and proactive monitoring. Focus on your business, not your server.

Is there a service level agreement?

Business and Enterprise plans include a 99.99% uptime SLA with financial credits. All plans are backed by our 30-day money-back guarantee.
