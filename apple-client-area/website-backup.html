Frequently asked questions.

Is CodeGuard free to manage?
CodeGuard plans are all affordable. You can upgrade and switch plans at any time depending on your website size, and your credit is prorated automatically to the new plan.
Where are the backups stored?
Every backup is stored offsite across multiple redundant data centers so your data is safe even if your hosting provider has an outage.
Are the backups encrypted?
Yes. All backups are encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256.
Do you backup databases?
Absolutely. MySQL and PostgreSQL databases are backed up together with your file system, so restores always recover a consistent site.
What is File Change Alert Monitoring?
Every daily snapshot is diffed against the previous one. If unexpected file changes are detected, you get a detailed email flagging exactly what changed.
What happens if my site gets infected?
Roll back to the last clean backup with one click. Your site is restored in minutes — without losing the most recent legitimate changes.
