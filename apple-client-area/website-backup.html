How backup works.

01

Connect your site

Enter your website URL and hosting credentials. Our system connects securely via SFTP or cPanel API — setup takes under 2 minutes.

02

We back up everything

Files, databases, emails, and configurations are captured daily. Every backup is a full snapshot, not just incremental changes, ensuring complete restore capability.

03

Monitor for changes

Our file monitoring system tracks every change to your site. Get instant alerts if files are modified unexpectedly — a key early indicator of compromise.

04

Restore anytime

Pick any backup point from your retention period and restore with one click. Restore the full site or individual files and databases selectively.

Backup FAQ.

Common questions about our backup service.

What gets backed up?
Everything — all website files, MySQL/PostgreSQL databases, email accounts, and server configurations. Each backup is a complete snapshot of your entire site.
How long are backups retained?
Retention depends on your plan: Basic keeps 7 days, Pro keeps 30 days, and Business keeps 90 days of daily backup history.
Can I restore individual files?
Yes! You can restore the entire site or browse individual files and databases within any backup. Selective restore lets you recover just what you need.
Does it work with any host?
Our backup service works with any hosting provider that supports SFTP, SSH, or cPanel. It's completely platform-agnostic and works alongside your existing setup.
Is my backup data encrypted?
Absolutely. All backups are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Your data is stored in geographically redundant facilities for maximum safety.
