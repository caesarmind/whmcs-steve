Email questions.

Answers about our professional email service.

Can I use my own domain?
Yes! Professional Email works with any domain you own. Simply update your MX records and start sending from you@yourdomain.com within minutes.
Which email clients are supported?
All standard email clients — Outlook, Apple Mail, Thunderbird, Gmail app, and any client that supports IMAP, POP3, or Exchange ActiveSync.
How much storage do I get?
Storage depends on your plan: Email Lite includes 10 GB, Email Pro includes 50 GB, and Email Suite includes 100 GB per mailbox.
Is there spam protection?
Every plan includes enterprise-grade anti-spam and anti-virus filtering. Over 99.9% of spam is blocked before it reaches your inbox.
