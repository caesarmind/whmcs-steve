How quickly will I see results from using SEO tools?
SEO is a long-term strategy. While our tools provide immediate insights and recommendations, most users start seeing measurable improvements in their search rankings within 2 to 4 weeks of implementing the suggested changes. Significant results typically appear within 2 to 3 months.
Can I track keywords for multiple websites?
Yes. The Lite plan supports tracking keywords for a single website. The Pro plan allows up to 5 websites, and the Agency plan offers unlimited website tracking, making it ideal for agencies managing multiple client sites.
What search engines do you support?
Our SEO tools track rankings across Google, Bing, and Yahoo. Google ranking data is updated daily for Pro and Agency plans, and weekly for the Lite plan. We also support tracking for local search results and mobile-specific rankings.
Can I cancel or change my plan at any time?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
