Frequently Asked Questions

How does the spam filtering work?
Our spam filtering uses multiple layers of detection including Bayesian filtering, real-time blackhole lists, greylisting, content analysis, and machine learning algorithms. Emails identified as spam are moved to quarantine where you can review and release them if needed.
Will legitimate emails be blocked?
Our system has a near-zero false positive rate. In the rare event a legitimate email is flagged, it will appear in your quarantine dashboard where you can release it with one click and whitelist the sender to prevent future occurrences.
How do I set up email filtering for my domain?
Setup is simple. After ordering, you will receive MX record details to update at your domain registrar. Once the DNS changes propagate (usually within a few hours), all email will be routed through our filtering servers automatically.
Can I manage spam settings for individual mailboxes?
Yes. The quarantine dashboard allows per-user settings including custom whitelists and blacklists, spam sensitivity levels, and notification preferences. Domain administrators can also set global policies that apply to all mailboxes.
