Frequently Asked Questions

What is an SSL certificate and why do I need one?
An SSL certificate encrypts the connection between your website and its visitors, protecting sensitive data like passwords, credit card numbers, and personal information. It also displays the padlock icon in browsers, building trust with your audience. Most modern browsers now warn visitors when a site lacks SSL.
What is the difference between DV, OV, and EV certificates?
Domain Validation (DV) verifies domain ownership only and is issued within minutes. Organization Validation (OV) additionally verifies your organization's identity, taking 1-3 days. Extended Validation (EV) provides the highest level of trust with rigorous verification of your company, displaying the full company name in the browser's address bar.
How long does it take to get my SSL certificate?
DV certificates are typically issued within minutes of completing domain verification. OV certificates take 1-3 business days as the certificate authority verifies your organization. EV certificates require the most thorough verification and typically take 3-5 business days.
Will my SSL certificate renew automatically?
Yes, all our SSL certificates support auto-renewal. You will receive a reminder email 30 days before expiration, and the certificate will renew automatically if you have a valid payment method on file. You can manage auto-renewal settings from your client area dashboard.
