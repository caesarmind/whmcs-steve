Can I use my existing domain for email?
Yes, you can use any domain you own. During setup, we will provide you with the DNS records (MX, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC) that you need to add to your domain's DNS settings. Our setup wizard walks you through the entire process step by step.
How do I migrate my existing emails?
We offer a free migration tool that transfers all your existing emails, contacts, and calendar events from your previous email provider. The migration runs in the background and typically completes within a few hours, depending on the amount of data.
Is there a limit on the number of mailboxes?
There is no hard limit on the number of mailboxes you can create. Each mailbox is billed individually at the plan rate. You can add or remove mailboxes at any time from your account dashboard, and billing adjusts automatically.
What spam and security protections are included?
All plans include enterprise-grade spam filtering, virus scanning, phishing protection, and encryption in transit. Business and Enterprise plans add advanced threat protection, including attachment sandboxing and link scanning for real-time protection against zero-day threats.
