Scheduled Network Upgrade — London DC

Posted Apr 10, 2026 · Alex from Hostnodes · 3 min read

We will be performing a scheduled network upgrade across our London data centre on April 20, 2026 between 02:00 and 06:00 UTC.

During this maintenance window, customers with services hosted in our London facility may experience brief intermittent connectivity disruptions. We expect each individual interruption to last no more than 2–3 minutes.

What's being upgraded

  • Core network switches upgraded to 400G
  • Redundant fibre paths between racks
  • Updated BGP routing configuration
  • New DDoS mitigation hardware installed

What you need to do

No action is required on your part. All services will remain accessible throughout the maintenance window, though you may notice brief latency spikes.

If your application requires continuous connectivity (e.g. financial transactions or real-time games) we recommend failing over to a secondary region for the duration of the window.

Affected regions

  • London (LHR1, LHR2)
  • Manchester (edge POP only)

Services in our US, Singapore, and Frankfurt regions are not affected.

If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to open a support ticket. Our team is monitoring the rollout in real time and will post updates to status.hostnodes.com.
