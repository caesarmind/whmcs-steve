SSL questions answered.

What's the difference between DV, OV, and EV?

DV validates domain ownership only. OV verifies your organization's identity. EV provides the highest level of trust with extensive verification and displays your company name in browsers.

How long does issuance take?

DV certificates are issued in 5 minutes. OV takes 1-3 business days. EV requires 3-5 business days due to thorough organizational verification.

Do I need a dedicated IP?

No. Modern servers support SNI (Server Name Indication), which allows multiple SSL certificates on a single IP address. Dedicated IPs are no longer required.

Will SSL slow down my site?

No. Modern SSL/TLS adds negligible overhead. In fact, HTTPS enables HTTP/2 which can actually make your site faster through multiplexing and header compression.

What is a Wildcard certificate?

A Wildcard SSL secures your main domain and all subdomains (e.g., *.example.com). One certificate covers mail.example.com, shop.example.com, and any other subdomain.

Is auto-renewal available?

Yes. Enable auto-renewal in your dashboard and your certificate will be renewed and reinstalled automatically before it expires. No manual intervention required.
