What is a VPN?
A virtual private network (VPN) gives your internet the privacy needed for a secure online experience. NordVPN encrypts your data and masks your IP address so you can surf the web without exposing your identity.
Why do I need a VPN?
Public Wi-Fi, ISP tracking, targeted advertising, geo-blocked content — a VPN solves all of these. It’s the single easiest way to take control of your online privacy.
How many devices can I use?
One NordVPN account covers up to 6 devices simultaneously — or configure it on your router to protect everything on your network.
Is NordVPN safe?
Yes. NordVPN uses AES-256 encryption and follows a strict no-logs policy — independently audited. Your activity is never tracked, collected, or shared.
What is DNS Leak Protection?
DNS Leak Protection prevents your DNS requests from escaping the encrypted VPN tunnel, keeping your browsing private even if your network or OS tries to bypass the VPN.
What is NordVPN Threat Protection?
Threat Protection is an add-on that blocks malicious ads, trackers, and malware-hosting sites — even when the VPN is not active. It works at the device level, so every app benefits.
What is Double VPN?
Double VPN routes your traffic through two separate VPN servers instead of one — encrypting your data twice for an extra layer of privacy.
How long does setup take?
Under 2 minutes. Install the app on your device, sign in with your WHMCS credentials, and click Connect. Done.
Which platforms are supported?
Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, Linux — plus browser extensions for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.
