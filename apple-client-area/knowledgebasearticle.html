Installing WordPress with one click

Last updated Mar 15, 2026 · Getting Started · 2 min read

WordPress can be installed on any of our hosting plans with just a few clicks. This guide walks you through the process using our built-in auto-installer.

Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have an active hosting plan and a domain name pointed to our nameservers. You'll also need access to your cPanel — you can reach it from My Services.

Installation steps

  1. Log into your cPanel dashboard from My Services.
  2. Scroll down to the Software section and click WordPress Manager.
  3. Click the Install button and select your domain from the dropdown.
  4. Choose your preferred admin username and a strong password.
  5. Click Install and wait approximately 30 seconds for completion.

After installation

Once installed, you can access your WordPress admin panel at yourdomain.com/wp-admin. We recommend installing an SSL certificate and updating all plugins to their latest versions.

If you encounter any issues during installation, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

You can also explore our related guides on securing a WordPress site and optimising performance.
