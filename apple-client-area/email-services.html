Frequently asked questions.

How does it work?
Email comes in and is routed through the SpamExperts self-learning servers. Every message is scored against a massive global threat database and spam patterns, then either delivered clean, quarantined, or discarded — so only safe mail reaches your inbox.
How accurate is the filtering?
SpamExperts delivers 99.9% spam detection with a near-zero false-positive rate. The self-learning engine adapts to new patterns daily.
Can I recover messages that got blocked?
Every blocked message sits in your quarantine for 14 days. Review, release, or whitelist the sender from the portal.
How long does setup take?
Less than 10 minutes. Point your domain’s MX records at the SpamExperts servers and filtering kicks in immediately.
How much email can I store?
Incoming and Outgoing plans include unlimited filtering throughput. Archiving plans offer 10 GB per mailbox with configurable retention.
