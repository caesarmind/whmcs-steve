Common questions.

What is SiteLock VPN?

SiteLock is a market leader in website security. SiteLock VPN lets your clients browse the web safely with encryption, IP masking, and a strict no-logs policy.

What is a VPN?

A VPN creates an encrypted tunnel between your device and the internet. It hides your IP, encrypts your traffic, and lets you browse privately on any network.

How does it work?

Install the app, sign in, and pick a server. Your connection is instantly encrypted and routed through the server of your choice.

How many devices?

A single account protects up to 6 devices simultaneously. You can also configure it on your router to cover every device on your network.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. Every plan includes a 30-day money-back guarantee — contact support for a full refund within 30 days, no questions asked.

Does it keep logs?

No. SiteLock VPN follows a strict no-logs policy — your browsing history is never tracked, stored, or shared.
