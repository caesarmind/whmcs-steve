Frequently asked questions.

What is XOVI?
XOVI is powered by Boxweb Digital Distribution. It’s a multimodal SEO advisory, content-generation tool, project-management platform and campaign tracker — built for modern teams who want measurable ranking growth. You can use XOVI to identify valuable keywords based on research data, optimize your site for better rankings, improve your content’s quality, and track progress.
Why do I need SEO?
Search is the #1 source of website traffic. Good SEO means more customers, more sales, and less ad spend.
When SEO no for me?
If you’re running a completely internal or private site with no public audience, SEO is not relevant. Otherwise, every public site benefits.
What is keyword optimization?
Keyword optimization is the process of researching, picking, and aligning content on your page around the search terms your target audience actually uses.
What is project visibility?
Project visibility is a composite score of how often your domain appears in search results for your tracked keywords — the higher the better.
