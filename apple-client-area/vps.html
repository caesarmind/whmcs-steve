Common questions.

What OS can I run?

Choose from Ubuntu, Debian, CentOS, Fedora, Arch Linux, or bring your own ISO. All major distributions are available with one-click deployment.

Do I get root access?

Yes, every VPS comes with full root access via SSH. You have complete control over your server environment, packages, and configuration.

Can I scale resources?

Resize CPU, RAM, and storage at any time with zero downtime. Changes apply instantly and billing adjusts automatically.

Is there DDoS protection?

Enterprise-grade DDoS mitigation is included with every VPS at no extra cost. We handle attacks up to 1 Tbps automatically.

How fast is provisioning?

New VPS instances deploy in under 55 seconds. Your server is online and ready for SSH access almost immediately.

What about backups?

Create on-demand snapshots with one click. Automatic weekly backups are included, and you can restore to any snapshot instantly.
