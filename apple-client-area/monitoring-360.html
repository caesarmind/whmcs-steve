Frequently asked questions.

What is Website Monitoring?
Website Monitoring tests websites for uptime, performance, and overall function. It verifies your site is up, running, and accessible for visitors so you can resolve issues before they impact business.
How does Website Monitoring work?
We run tests at regular intervals (every 1, 5, or 10 minutes depending on your plan). When an issue is detected, 360 Monitoring sends an alert and records the issue for later analysis.
How does Performance Monitoring work?
Performance Monitoring checks system resources like CPU, memory, disk, I/O, and network uptime to ensure everything runs smoothly — and catches regressions before they hurt UX.
How does SSL Monitoring work?
With SSL Monitoring you track certificate validity and plan ahead for expirations, ensuring seamless certificate management and no surprise browser warnings.
What locations are supported for monitoring?
Monitor from 27 locations worldwide including Australia, Canada, Finland, France, Germany, Japan, Mexico, the Netherlands, Sweden, the UK, and the US.
What is Full Site Check?
Full Site Check crawls your entire website or store and identifies dead links, broken images, JavaScript errors, and many more anomalies — helping you catch issues before visitors do.
Are there extra fees for notifications?
All paid plans include unlimited multi-channel alerts. Some carriers may charge additional fees for SMS messages or mobile data.
Can I upgrade my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time from the client area.
