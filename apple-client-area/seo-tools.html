SEO questions.

How often are rankings updated?

Keyword rankings are checked daily and updated in your dashboard each morning. You'll also receive weekly email reports with position changes and trends.

Which search engines are tracked?

We track Google, Bing, Yahoo, and 100+ regional and specialized search engines. You can configure which engines matter most for your target audience.

Can I track competitors?

Yes! Add competitor domains and we'll track their rankings for the same keywords. Compare positions side-by-side and identify opportunities to outrank them.

What does the site audit check?

Our crawler checks 200+ SEO factors including broken links, missing meta tags, duplicate content, page speed, mobile-friendliness, schema markup, and more.

Do you offer white-label reports?

Enterprise plans include fully white-label PDF reports with your logo and branding. Perfect for agencies presenting results to their clients.

Is there an API?

Enterprise plans include full REST API access for programmatic access to keyword data, audit results, and competitor intelligence. Documentation included.
