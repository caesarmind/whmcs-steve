Domain questions.

Quick answers about registering and managing domains.

How long does domain registration take?
Most domains are registered instantly. Some premium or restricted TLDs may require additional verification, which typically takes 1-3 business days.
Can I transfer my domain here?
Yes! We accept transfers from any registrar. You'll need your domain's authorization code (EPP code). The transfer process usually completes within 5-7 days.
Is WHOIS privacy included?
WHOIS privacy protection is included free with every domain registration. Your personal contact details are hidden from public WHOIS lookups automatically.
What happens when my domain expires?
We'll send multiple reminder emails before expiration. If it expires, there's a 30-day grace period to renew at the standard rate. After that, a redemption fee may apply.
