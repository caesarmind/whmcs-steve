Builder questions.

Do I need coding skills?

Not at all. The Website Builder is 100% visual drag-and-drop. Everything is point-and-click — no HTML, CSS, or JavaScript knowledge required.

Can I sell products?

Yes! Pro and Business plans include e-commerce features — product catalog, shopping cart, checkout, payment processing, and inventory management.

Are templates mobile-friendly?

Every template is fully responsive and looks great on mobile, tablet, and desktop. You can also preview and customize the mobile view separately.

Can I use my own domain?

Absolutely. Connect any domain you own, or register a new one through us. Free SSL is included with every site regardless of plan.
